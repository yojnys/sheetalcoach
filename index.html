About Me

Hi, I’m Sheetal Soni—an ICF-certified coach and senior HR leader with over 25 years of experience enabling individuals, leaders, and teams across diverse cultures and regions to unlock their full potential.

My coaching style is intuitive, strengths-based, and non-directive. I hold space with deep listening, curiosity, and presence, creating a safe, reflective environment where insight and transformation can naturally unfold.

With experience working across Asia, Europe, and North America, I bring a deep understanding of cross-cultural dynamics and global business environments.

I’ve coached:

  • Senior leaders and high-potential talent
  • Professionals at crossroads
  • Teens and young adults
  • People facing life transitions

My Core Beliefs:

  • You are whole, capable, and resourceful.
  • Coaching is a space for insight—not instruction.
  • Real change happens when reflection meets action.

About Coaching

Coaching is a powerful, forward-focused partnership that supports growth, clarity, and meaningful change—across all areas of life.

It’s not about giving advice or fixing problems. Instead, coaching creates space for deeper self-awareness, clarity, and forward movement.

As your coach, I’m here to:

  • Listen deeply and without judgment
  • Ask thought-provoking questions
  • Help you uncover insights, patterns, and purpose
  • Support you in taking intentional, aligned steps forward

  3. Clarity Sessions
  4. Coaching for Students / Teenagers
